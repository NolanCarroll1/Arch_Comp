About Us

Video Transcript

Hi and thank you for choosing Carroll Architecture. Here at Carroll Architecture, we pride ourselves on being a long list of descendants of hardworking individuals. We all started back all the way back in the 1800s when my great-great-grandfather Edgar came across the seas from Denmark. Here in the United States, he didn't know so much as the names of the tools he was using in English but what he did know how to do was make things that brought people together. He knew how to work hard. Here at Carroll Architecture, we uphold that tradition in trying to make places where you and your descendants can gather for generations to come in comfort.
